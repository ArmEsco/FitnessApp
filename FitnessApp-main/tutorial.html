Welcome to the NABZ Fitness Tutorial

This guide will walk you through the key features and functionality of the app to help you get started.

Step 1: Sign Up

Go to the Sign-Up page and fill in your details to create an account.

Step 2: Log In

Use your credentials on the Login page to access your dashboard.

Step 3: Track Your Habits

In the Dashboard page add your daily, weekly, or monthly goals, monitor your progress with the chart, add custom reminders to stay on track with your goals, and earn badges for completing your goals.

Step 4: View the Leaderboard

Check out the Leaderboard page to see how you rank against other users and teams.

Step 5: Add reminders

Add reminders at the top of the page
1: Click the Add Reminders button.
2: Type in the reminder you want and click OK.
3: Hover over the View Reminders to look at your reminders.

Step 6: Earn Badges

Reach your goals to earn digital badges and boost your motivation! There is a total of 8 badges that you can earn.

Step 7: Look at your progress

The chart keeps track of your progress and helps you stay on top of goals!

Step 8: Add habits you want to follow

Choose a date, type in the habits you want to follow, and stay on track with that habit by being able to visually see that habit everyday in the habit overview!
